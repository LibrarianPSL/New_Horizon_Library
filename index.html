June Literary Game: Hogwarts Spell Hunt 🪄

Monthly Activity

This Month in the Library:
We are thrilled to engage our students with the Hogwarts Spell Hunt! As shown on our physical library noticeboard, children are presented with 7 mysterious Harry Potter spells.

How the activity works:
Students are encouraged to research what these spells are and their magical effects. They write their answers on a piece of paper, including their Name and Grade, and drop it into the submission box located in the library.

This activity runs throughout the month of June. At the end of the month, the names and answers of the successful participants will be proudly displayed on the library noticeboard to celebrate their investigative skills! ⚡

Activity Period: June 1 - June 30, 2026

About Our Library

Inspiring creativity, focus, and a lifelong love for learning

Library Organization

Our library collection is carefully classified using the Dewey Decimal Classification (DDC) system and sorted alphabetically. This structured approach helps students effortlessly learn subject categories while easily locating their favorite reads.

Enriching Monthly Activities

To enrich young minds, we organize 12 exciting, grade-wide activities annually—one every month alongside regular book issuances. These events are designed to spark imagination and boost focus. Some of our favorites include:

  • Book & Author Talks: Deep dives into fascinating literary worlds.
  • Dice Roll Story Relay: A thrilling circle game where a dice roll decides who continues the story. Stuck? Use one of your two lifelines to draw a random inspiration slip from the center!
  • Literary Tic-Tac-Toe: A life-sized board game where claiming a tile requires completing a fun, book-related task.
  • Character Cut-Out Bookmark Making: Letting creativity shine through hands-on crafting.
  • Book Character Debates: Defend your champion based on their unique talents and powers. Imagine Geronimo Stilton, the brilliant Italian mouse detective, going head-to-head with Naruto, the powerful ninja!
