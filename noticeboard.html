June Literary Game: Hogwarts Spell Hunt 🪄

Monthly Activity

This Month in the Library:
We are thrilled to engage our students with the Hogwarts Spell Hunt! As shown on our physical library noticeboard, children are presented with 7 mysterious Harry Potter spells.

How the activity works:
Students are encouraged to research what these spells are and their magical effects. They write their answers on a piece of paper, including their Name and Grade, and drop it into the submission box located in the library.

This activity runs throughout the month of June. At the end of the month, the names and answers of the successful participants will be proudly displayed on the library noticeboard to celebrate their investigative skills! ⚡

Activity Period: June 1 - June 30, 2026
