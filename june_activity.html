🎥 June Activity

Our featured activity for this month

Dice Roll Relay

In this engaging Dice Roll Relay activity, children are divided into groups of six, with each child assigned a number from 1 to 6.

When the dice is rolled, the student with the matching number starts a story with just one sentence. The student sitting opposite to them then writes that sentence down on paper.

The story must follow a structured format with the following elements, built sentence-by-sentence with each dice roll:

  • How the story begins
  • The main character
  • The setting
  • The problem
  • The twist
  • The solution

This relay continues as children roll the dice and creatively build upon the previous sentence until a complete, collaborative story is formed. It's a fantastic way to boost creativity, encourage coordination, and keep everyone thoroughly engaged while having fun!
